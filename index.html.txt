## Brand & Style

This design system is built for a discerning female audience that values curation, quality, and aesthetic harmony. The brand personality is "The Modern Tastemaker"—authoritative yet gentle, sophisticated yet accessible. 

The visual style is **Soft Minimalism**. It avoids the sterility of traditional tech minimalism by introducing warmth through pastel tones and metallic accents. The interface prioritizes negative space to allow product imagery to breathe, mimicking the layout of high-end print magazines like *Vogue* or *Kinfolk*. We employ a "Quiet Luxury" aesthetic where the interface recedes to let the curated content and elegant typography lead the user experience.

## Colors

The palette is rooted in a high-brightness, low-saturation foundation to evoke a sense of calm and cleanliness.

*   **Primary (Soft Pink):** Used for soft background blocks, category tags, and subtle hover states. It provides the "feminine" warmth without being overwhelming.
*   **Secondary (Sophisticated Gold):** Reserved for high-value actions, interactive accents, and decorative borders. It serves as the "jewelry" of the UI.
*   **Tertiary/Background (White):** The dominant color to ensure a "clean" and "airy" feeling.
*   **Neutral (Charcoal):** Instead of pure black, a soft charcoal (#4A4A4A) is used for body text to maintain a premium, low-contrast reading experience that reduces eye strain.

## Typography

The typographic strategy relies on a high-contrast pairing between a classic serif and a modern geometric sans-serif.

*   **Playfair Display** is used exclusively for editorial headings. It should be typeset with slightly tighter letter-spacing for large displays to emphasize its elegant thick-and-thin strokes.
*   **Montserrat** provides a functional balance. For body text, we use a generous line height (1.6) to enhance readability and contribute to the "airy" vibe. 
*   **Labels and Metadata** (like "Amazon Choice" or "Best Seller") should use Montserrat in Uppercase with expanded letter-spacing to create a sophisticated, branded feel.

## Layout & Spacing

The design system utilizes a **Fixed-Width Centered Grid** for desktop and a **Fluid Grid** for mobile.

*   **Desktop:** A 12-column grid with a maximum container width of 1280px. Large margins (80px) ensure the content feels like an intentional showcase.
*   **Tablet:** Shifts to an 8-column grid with 40px margins.
*   **Mobile:** A 4-column grid with 20px margins. 
*   **Rhythm:** We follow an 8px base unit. Section vertical padding is intentionally generous (scaling from 64px to 120px) to prevent the "cluttered storefront" look typical of affiliate sites. Content should be grouped logically with clear white space "buffers" between unrelated sections.

## Elevation & Depth

To maintain a minimalist aesthetic, this design system avoids heavy shadows. Depth is communicated through **Tonal Layering** and **Subtle Outlines**.

*   **Surfaces:** The primary background is pure White (#FFFFFF). Secondary content blocks or "drawers" use the Soft Pink (#FCE4EC) at a very low opacity or as a solid fill to denote a change in context.
*   **Shadows:** When necessary (e.g., for hovering over a product card), use an "Ambient Glow"—a very soft, diffused shadow with a hint of gold: `0px 10px 30px rgba(212, 175, 55, 0.08)`.
*   **Dividers:** Use hairline borders (1px) in Soft Pink or a very faint Gold for horizontal rules to separate content without creating visual noise.

## Shapes

The shape language is **Soft and Tailored**. We avoid perfectly sharp corners to stay approachable, but avoid heavy "pill" shapes to maintain a professional, high-fashion editorial feel.

*   **Components:** Buttons and input fields use a standard 4px (0.25rem) radius.
*   **Imagery:** Product photos should use a consistent 8px (0.5rem) radius.
*   **Callouts:** Promotional banners or "Editor's Pick" badges may use a slightly more rounded 12px corner to distinguish them from functional UI elements.

## Components

*   **Buttons:** The primary button is a solid Gold (#D4AF37) with white text, using Montserrat Bold in uppercase. The secondary button is an "Outlined" style using a Gold border and Gold text.
*   **Product Cards:** Minimalist frames with a focus on high-quality photography. The price is displayed in Montserrat Medium, while the product title is Playfair Display. No heavy borders; use the Ambient Glow on hover.
*   **Price Tags:** Use a Soft Pink background with charcoal text to make them visible but non-aggressive.
*   **Input Fields:** Clean white backgrounds with a 1px Soft Pink border that transitions to Gold on focus.
*   **Affiliate Disclaimer:** Stylized as a small, italicized Montserrat label at the bottom of cards or top of pages, ensuring transparency while maintaining the premium aesthetic.
*   **Navigation:** A centered logo with a top-level menu in Montserrat (Label-md style). Use a Gold underline for active states.
